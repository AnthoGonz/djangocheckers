Django Checkers Tutorial

Welcome to Django Checkers! Above is a quick diagram to show the layout and controls.

The yellow square represents the square that you selected, and the green square represents the square that you want to move. You must press the "Make Move" button to make your move!

To jump a square, just select the square after the piece. If you click and there is no green square, that means the move is not valid!

To double jump, you just have to keep clicking! If the move is valid, it will keep making green squares!

If the double jump is straight, you can use a shortcut by just clicking the last square. You can still use the previous method to double jump.

If you reach the end of the board, your pieces becomes a King, and move forwards and backwards!
